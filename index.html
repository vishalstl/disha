An AI powered next generation analytics platform leverages AI algorithms to analyze the vast data across different sources and identify patterns, trends, and insights.to provide a consolidated view and recommendations on organizational performance.

With Disha, management can access real-time insights and metrics to monitor key performance indicators, daily/weekly production, identify trends, and make informed decisions quickly at group level across departments. Additionally, Disha enables greater collaboration across departments,
